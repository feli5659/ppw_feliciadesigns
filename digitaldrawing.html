project description

A couple of years prior to the corona pandemic, I was working on cruise ships in the US as a singer and performer, and though it was hard work - we also had a fair bit of free time, when we weren’t rehearsing or performing.

I therefore picked up my apple pen, opened procreate and watched a bunched of YouTube videos to better my drawing skills. I loved playing with colors and exploring my creative side. I am the type of person, that goes ALL-IN on hobbies I’m passionate about. It was this that made me realize, a career in design was what I wanted to do.
