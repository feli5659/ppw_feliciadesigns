about

My name is Felicia Hetman, and I’m a passionate frontend designer currently studying multimedia design at KEA. As a design-driven individual, I’ve always been drawn to the world of digital design and how it can be used to enhance user experiences.

Throughout my studies, I’ve developed a strong skill set in areas such as web development, the design process, user interface design and graphic design. I’m constantly seeking out new challenges and opportunities to improve my abilities, both in and out of the classroom.

I love the idea of having to stay on top of the latest trends and technologies and apply them to my work. I believe that staying up to date with the latest developments is key to creating truly innovative and engaging designs that stand out from the competition.

Whether it’s working on a new website or creating a digital campaign, I’m always excited to tackle new projects and bring my creative vision to life. I’m eager to join a team of like-minded professionals who share my passion for design and are committed to pushing the boundaries of what’s possible.

In short, I’m a frontend designer with a love for all things digital. I’m hungry to learn more and excited to see where my career takes me.
