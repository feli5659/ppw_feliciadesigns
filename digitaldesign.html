digital design

project description

This project was the exam at the end of our second semester. I was a part of a hardworking and passionate team of individuals, and we re-designed the clients website in Wordpress, and created a completely new brand identity across several different platforms. We worked iteratively with the Design Thinking method, and created a custom Wordpress theme to create a solution very close to our initial mockup.

We received the highest grade possible for this project, and I gained knowledge in coding in php, working in Wordpress and working as a part of a team. I also enjoyed the whole design process and creating designs based on the research we had carried out.
